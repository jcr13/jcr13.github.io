IRACDA Journal Club - Flipped vs. Alternative Active Learning

October 12, 2016

The cohort of IRACDA postdoctoral scholars at Stony Brook meets each month to discuss a journal article related to pedagogy in undergraduate science. This month we discussed "Comparing the Efficacy of Flipped vs. Alternative Active Learning in a College Genetics Course" by Thomas A. Mennella.
